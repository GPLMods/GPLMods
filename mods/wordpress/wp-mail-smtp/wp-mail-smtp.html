About This Plugin

Is your WordPress site not sending emails? You’re not alone. 3+ million websites use WP Mail SMTP to send emails reliably.

What is SMTP?

SMTP (Simple Mail Transfer Protocol) is an industry standard for sending emails. Proper SMTP configuration helps increase email deliverability by using authentication to prove your emails are genuine, preventing them from landing in the spam folder.

How Does WP Mail SMTP Work?

This plugin fixes your email delivery problems by reconfiguring WordPress to use a proper SMTP provider. It includes many different mailer options like SendLayer, SMTP.com, Brevo (Sendinblue), Mailgun, SendGrid, and more.

WP Mail SMTP Pro Features

  • Email Log: Keep a record of all emails sent from your WordPress site to audit, resend, or test delivery.
  • Email Reports & Tracking: Track your email deliverability and engagement with open and click-through rates.
  • Email Alerts: Get notified instantly via Slack, SMS, or email if your site stops sending emails.
  • Backup Connection: Configure a secondary mailer that kicks in if your primary connection fails.
  • Smart Conditional Routing: Send different types of emails using different mailers to improve deliverability.
