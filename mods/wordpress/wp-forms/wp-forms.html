About This Plugin

WPForms is the most beginner-friendly drag & drop WordPress form builder. Create contact forms, payment forms, surveys, and other types of forms for your site in minutes with just a few clicks!

Drag & Drop Form Builder

Create custom forms in minutes with the easy-to-use drag and drop online form builder. With over 2000+ pre-built form templates, you can get started quickly without hiring a developer.

Pro Features & Addons

  • Payment Forms: Easily accept credit card payments with Stripe, PayPal, Square, and Authorize.Net integrations.
  • Surveys & Polls: Create custom survey forms with smart fields like Likert scales and star ratings, complete with interactive reporting.
  • Smart Conditional Logic: Show or hide fields based on user selections to create intelligent, interactive forms.
  • User Registration & Post Submissions: Create custom login forms and allow users to submit guest posts or testimonials.
  • Form Landing Pages: Create distraction-free custom form landing pages to boost conversions.
