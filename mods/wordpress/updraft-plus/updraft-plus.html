About This Plugin

UpdraftPlus is the world’s highest-ranking and most popular scheduled backup and migration plugin, trusted by over 3 million websites to backup, restore, and migrate their WordPress sites.

Backup, Restore, and Migrate

Easily backup your site to a huge number of remote storage locations like Dropbox, Google Drive, and Amazon S3. Restore your entire website—or individual components like plugins and themes—in just a few clicks.

UpdraftPlus Premium Features

  • Incremental Backups: Only backup changes to your files, saving time and server resources.
  • Automatic Backups Before Updates: Automatically run a backup before any WordPress, plugin, or theme update.
  • More Storage Options: Get access to premium locations like OneDrive, Backblaze, Google Cloud, and WebDAV.
  • Advanced Control: Run backups at specific times, backup non-WordPress files, and encrypt your database.
