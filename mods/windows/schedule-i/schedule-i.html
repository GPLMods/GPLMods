About This Game

From a small-time drug dealer to a kingpin, you arrive in a new town with no cash, no product, and no connections. Your mission is to build a drug empire from the ground up in the grungy west-coast city of Hyland Point.

Manufacture & Distribute

Produce a range of drugs to satisfy your customers, each with its own unique production process. Discover special recipes to create new variants with powerful properties.

Expand Your Empire

  • Walk, skate, or drive throughout Hyland Point to get product into the hands of customers.
  • Purchase new properties and hire employees to expand the scope and scale of your operations.
  • Work with suppliers to build a fully automated production and distribution chain.
