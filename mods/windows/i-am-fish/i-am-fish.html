About This Game

I Am Fish is a charming, physics-based adventure starring four intrepid fish friends, forcibly separated from their home in a pet shop fish tank. Swim, fly, roll and chomp your way to the open ocean in a bid for freedom and to re-unite once again.

FINTASTIC FRIENDS

Meet our heroes! Goldfish, Pufferfish, Piranha, and Flying Fish. These plucky heroes will leave no bowl unturned, putting their heart and shoal into the mission to re-unite!

THINK OUTSIDE THE BOWL

With its idyllic coastline and quaint villages, Barnardshire might seem tranquil, but to our aquatic adventurers, it’s alive with threats—crossing roads, dodging deep fat fryers, and avoiding cantankerous locals.

SWIM TO FREEDOM

Our four fin-tastic friends will hitch rides in jars, mop buckets, and even the occasional pint glass. Navigating in each make-shift aquatic vehicle poses its own unique challenge as you search for the shimmering, open ocean.
