Mod Information

This modded version of Spotify unlocks premium features on your iOS device:

  • Premium Unlocked: Enables all Spotify Premium features for free.
  • No Ads: Blocks all audio, video, and banner ads for an uninterrupted listening experience.
  • Unlimited Skips: Skip as many songs as you want without limitation.
  • High-Quality Streaming: Listen to music in Very High and Lossless audio quality.

About This App

SpotC++ is a modified version of the official Spotify app for iOS. It's an IPA file that can be sideloaded onto non-jailbroken devices to provide the full Spotify Premium experience without a subscription.
