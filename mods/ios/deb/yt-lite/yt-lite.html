Tweak Features

This iOS deb file enhances the official YouTube app with tons of new features:

  • Advanced Downloader: Download videos, audio, thumbnails, posts, and profile pictures.
  • UI Customization: Remove feed elements, reorder tabs, enable OLED mode, and more.
  • Enhanced Player: Use gestures, set default quality, and choose preferred audio tracks.
  • Built-in SponsorBlock: Automatically skips sponsor segments in videos.
  • Settings Management: Save, load, and restore your custom settings.

About This Tweak

YTLite is a powerful tweak that enhances the official YouTube app with a massive suite of new features. It adds advanced downloading capabilities, deep UI customization, an enhanced video player, and much more. YTLite's preferences can be found directly within the official YouTube app's settings menu.
