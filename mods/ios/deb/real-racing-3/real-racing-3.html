Tweak Features

This iOS jailbreak tweak adds a powerful mod menu to the Real Racing 3 app:

  • Freeze Currency: Your Gold and R$ will not decrease when you spend them.
  • Free Shopping: Buy currency packs from the in-game store for free.
  • Universal Support: Works for both the USA and Worldwide (non-USA) versions of the game.
  • All Cars Unlocked: Access every car in the game from the start.

About This Tweak

This is a jailbreak tweak that injects a mod menu into the official Real Racing 3 game from the App Store. It gives you access to unlimited currency and all cars, allowing you to enjoy the ultimate racing experience without the grind.
